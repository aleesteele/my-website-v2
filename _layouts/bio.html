I'm Anne Lee Steele, currently a MA candidate at the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies, Geneva.

Before moving to Switzerland, I researched conservation in Bhutan, led student expeditions in Nepal, and coded data visualizations for a Washington DC think tank. As an undergraduate at Columbia University, I studied global cities and natural resource conflict and designed media for student organizations in New York City. I'm interested in how technology and the environment affect people and their politics, and I've written about how important those in the middle are for translating such complicated problems. For me, this means working between disciplines, and using modern tools to make them accessible.
