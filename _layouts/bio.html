I'm Anne Lee Steele, currently a MA candidate at the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies, Geneva.

Before moving to Switzerland, I researched conservation in Bhutan, lead student expeditions in Nepal, and coded data visualizations for a Washington DC think tank. As an undergraduate at Columbia University, I studied global cities and natural resource conflict.

These experiences introduced me to my research interests, and how to use modern tools to help communicate them. My primary interest is in how technology and the environment affect people and their politics, and I've written about how those in the middle might be the keys for change.
